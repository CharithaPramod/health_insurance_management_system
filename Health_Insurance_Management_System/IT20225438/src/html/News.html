Healthy Life Insurance declares the highest ever health insurance bonus of Rs.8.2 billion

Colombo 12th August 2020: Healthy life insurance is proud to declare the largest ever health insurance bonus of Rs. 8.2 billion for the year 2020 to its health insurance policyholders, recording the largest health insurance bonus ever to be declared in the insurance industry of Sri Lanka, this brought total of life insurance bonuses paid to policyholders since 2005 to Rs. 64.6 billion. With this outstanding achievement Sri Lanka Insurance has upheld the trust placed by the policyholders by endorsing Healthy Life insurance as the health policy with the highest return. Educating the Sri Lankan people of the importance of a health insurance is an aspect Healthy Life Insurance holds close to its heart from the inception of its existence. It is in the forefront of propagating the concept of health insurance to every corner of the country. The trust Healthy Life Insurance has earned from people of Sri Lanka is evident by the accolades it received. HLIC is ranked ‘The Most Loved Insurance Brand’ and the 3rd ‘Most Loved Consumer Brand’ in the country by Brand Finance. Healthy Life insurance basket offers a range of health insurance plans to suit every stage and need of life in today’s competitive market environment. Recording the largest health insurance fund of Rs.116 billion, the company’s prudent investment management strategies and accumulating health fund have enabled the company to give higher returns to its policyholders. The company is on a mission of being a customer focused company which constantly innovates in providing insurance services to customers and is striving to increase dynamism and expertise of the overall operation of the company. Also the company is focused on expansion of the branch network which currently stands at 158 branches located island wide to provide a convenient service to customers.

Healthy Life Insurance and People’s Bank ties up in unique scheme for customer convenience

Colombo 27th February 2020: An agreement was signed between the two state giants HLIc & People’s Bank on 26th of February 2020 at the HLIC head office, setting the stage for an array of innovative products and services to the public. Agreement was exchanged by the two Chairmen Mr.Jagath Wellawatta Chairman of HLIC, Mr.Sujeewa Rajapakse Chairman of People’s Bank and the senior corporate management of both companies. HLIC and People’s Bank will open office units in each other’s office premises to provide convenience to the customers. A unique online Fire Insurance system and Title Insurance scheme will be offered for People’s Bank customers and a co-branded motor insurance policy with special features are also offered to People’s Bank customers by HLIC. A special payment gateway for premium settlement will be effective with this agreement. Both HLIC and People’s Bank employees too will benefit from the tie up. The agreement will facilitate integrated services of the banking sector and the insurance sector under one roof providing convenience for customers of both HLIC and People’s Bank.
